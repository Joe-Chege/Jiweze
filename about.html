About Jiweze Initiative

Jiweze Initiative is a dynamic organization committed to empowering young entrepreneurs in Kenya. We provide valuable resources, mentorship programs, and educational opportunities to help aspiring business owners turn their dreams into reality.

Our Team and Partners

Our dedicated team consists of experienced professionals in entrepreneurship, finance, and mentorship. We collaborate with a network of partners, including local businesses and educational institutions, to create a supportive ecosystem for young entrepreneurs.

History and Milestones

Jiweze Initiative was founded in 20XX with the goal of fostering economic growth and innovation among Kenyan youth. Over the years, we have achieved significant milestones, including:

  • Launching successful mentorship programs.
  • Helping hundreds of entrepreneurs start and grow their businesses.
  • Expanding our reach to communities across Kenya.
