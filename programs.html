Our Programs

At Jiweze Initiative, we offer a range of programs designed to empower young entrepreneurs and help them succeed in their ventures. Explore our available programs below:

  • Entrepreneurship Accelerator: A comprehensive program for early-stage entrepreneurs to refine their business ideas and accelerate growth.
  • Mentorship Network: Connect with experienced mentors who provide guidance, support, and industry insights.
  • Financial Literacy Workshops: Learn essential financial skills to manage your business's finances effectively.

Program Details

Each program at Jiweze Initiative is tailored to meet specific entrepreneurial needs. Below, you'll find program details, eligibility criteria, and the application process:

Entrepreneurship Accelerator

Program Overview: Our Entrepreneurship Accelerator is a 12-week intensive program designed for early-stage entrepreneurs. It includes mentorship, workshops, and access to resources.

Eligibility: Entrepreneurs with a business idea or startup in the ideation or early growth stage.

Application Process: To apply, complete our online application form and submit your business proposal.

Mentorship Network

Program Overview: Our Mentorship Network connects aspiring entrepreneurs with experienced mentors who provide guidance and support throughout their entrepreneurial journey.

Eligibility: Open to all young entrepreneurs looking for mentorship and guidance.

Application Process: Register for our Mentorship Network, and we'll match you with a suitable mentor based on your needs.

Financial Literacy Workshops

Program Overview: Our Financial Literacy Workshops are designed to equip entrepreneurs with essential financial skills, including budgeting, financial planning, and investment strategies.

Eligibility: Open to all young entrepreneurs and business enthusiasts interested in improving their financial knowledge.

Application Process: Simply sign up for our upcoming workshops through our website. Attendance is free.
